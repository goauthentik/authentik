termName: Single Logout (SLO)
---

<!-- shortDescription:begin -->

Terminates sessions across parties in a federation.

<!-- shortDescription:end -->
<!-- longDescription:begin -->

Coordinated logout that ends sessions at the Identity Provider (IdP) or OpenID Provider (OP) and participating Service Providers (SPs) or Relying Parties (RPs). Implemented via front-channel (browser) or back-channel (server) mechanisms depending on protocol support.

<!-- longDescription:end -->
