<!-- shortDescription:begin -->

PAP,CHAP,MS‑CHAPv2,and EAP methods.

<!-- shortDescription:end -->
<!-- longDescription:begin -->

RADIUS transports credential exchanges such as PAP (plaintext password), CHAP/MS‑CHAPv2 (challenge‑response), or EAP methods (e.g., PEAP, EAP‑TLS) terminated on the NAS or server.

<!-- longDescription:end -->
