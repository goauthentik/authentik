<!-- shortDescription:begin -->

Direct server-to-server communication.

<!-- shortDescription:end -->
<!-- longDescription:begin -->

Server-to-server interactions without user-agent involvement, e.g., token exchange at the token endpoint, introspection, revocation, or back-channel logout. More reliable for session coordination and avoids browser constraints.

<!-- longDescription:end -->
