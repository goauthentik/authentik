termName: Network Access Server (NAS)
---

<!-- shortDescription:begin -->

Device that sends RADIUS requests to the server.

<!-- shortDescription:end -->
<!-- longDescription:begin -->

Examples include VPN concentrators, Wi‑Fi controllers, firewalls, or switches. The NAS acts as the RADIUS client initiating Access‑Request and Accounting messages.

<!-- longDescription:end -->
