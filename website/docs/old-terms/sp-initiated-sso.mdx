termName: SP-initiated SSO

<!-- shortDescription:begin -->

SSO flow started at the Service Provider.

<!-- shortDescription:end -->
<!-- longDescription:begin -->

User starts at the SP, which sends an AuthnRequest (often via Redirect) to the IdP. After authentication, the IdP posts the Response to the SP's ACS, optionally preserving RelayState.

<!-- longDescription:end -->
