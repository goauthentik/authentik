<!-- shortDescription:begin -->

Declarative files to template and reconcile authentik config.

<!-- shortDescription:end -->
<!-- longDescription:begin -->

YAML-based configuration used to create and update objects (flows, providers, policies, etc.) as code. Blueprints can be applied from the filesystem, database, or OCI registries, support meta models for dependencies, and are reconciled periodically or on change.

<!-- longDescription:end -->
