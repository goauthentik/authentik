Transport mechanism for SAML messages.

<!-- shortDescription:end -->
<!-- longDescription:begin -->

Defines how SAML messages are transported. Common bindings are HTTP-Redirect and HTTP-POST for SSO, and SOAP/Artifact for back-channel or large payloads.
