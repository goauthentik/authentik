termName: Subject (sub)

<!-- shortDescription:begin -->

Unique identifier of the token's principal.

<!-- shortDescription:end -->
<!-- longDescription:begin -->

The 'sub' claim identifies the principal (end-user or client) represented by the token. In OIDC, 'sub' is stable per issuer and may be pairwise or public.

<!-- longDescription:end -->
